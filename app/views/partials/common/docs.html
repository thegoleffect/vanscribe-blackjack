♣ Blackjack

Instructions

Click a table name to join (if seats open)

Red circle opens action menu

H = Hit, St = Stand, D = Deal

Click the (i) to go back to Lobby


Rationale

Best played using an iPhone.

(Or shrink the width of your browser for maximum effect)

This game was designed, built, & deployed over a week to demonstrate a wide range of artistic and technical skills.

In addition to being a showcase, I forced myself to learn several new things at the cost of time (and possibly health). These cool toys are listed in the Colophon.

A few of the more annoying "Tracked but Incomplete" parts will get fixed while it is live... but after a power nap.
